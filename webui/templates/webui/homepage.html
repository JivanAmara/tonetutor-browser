Learning to pronounce the tones in Mandarin is one of the most difficult parts of learning Mandarin, second only to learning to write the characters themselves. ToneTutor is a simple tool for the beginning learner to get fast feedback on their pronunciation of tones for single pinyin syllables. This will give your learning a speed-boost through the initial challenges.

Don't spend too much time on one syllable. If you can't get the pronunciation right after 6-8 tries, move on. It will come up again after you've strengthened your skills.

In ToneTutor, since they don't provide the same challenge as pronouncing tones, the sounds themselves aren't graded, only your pronunciation of the tones. For example, if prompted with "jì" and you say "yòu" correctly you'll get a green light to move to the next. Stay tuned for a similar tool providing feedback on tone-pair pronunciation.

How does it work? Many recordings of native speakers pronouncing these syllables have been analyzed using a custom machine-learning algorithm to identify tones from audio files. This analysis results in a model which when given an audio file indicates which tone it is.
